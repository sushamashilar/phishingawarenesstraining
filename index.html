What is Phishing?

Phishing is a type of cyberattack where attackers trick individuals into revealing sensitive information by pretending to be trustworthy entities.

Common Signs of Phishing

  • Urgent or threatening language
  • Suspicious links or attachments
  • Requests for personal information
  • Generic greetings like "Dear Customer"
